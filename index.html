What is FreeAXE FlaskOS?

FreeAXE FlaskOS is a minimalist Linux distro built on top of Xubuntu, designed for power users, developers, and those who just want a clean, fast system without bloat.

Based in the USA, FlaskOS is a globally friendly distro, with localization available out of the box!
